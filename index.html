About

Qian Yaguan is a professor and master's student/doctoral supervisor. In 2014, he received his Ph.D. degree in computer science from Zhejiang University. He is currently the deputy dean of the School of Big Data, Zhejiang University of Science and Technology, the head of the big data discipline, the director of the Hikvision Edge Intelligent Security Joint Laboratory, and the head of the big data and artificial intelligence security team. He is a member of the Big Data Security and Privacy Computing Special Committee of the China Chinese Information Society, and a distinguished researcher of the Provincial Key Laboratory of Intelligent Internet of Things Network and Data Security. He is the review expert of Wu Wenjun Artificial Intelligence Science and Technology Award, the executive chairman of the International Symposium on Sensor Technology and Control (ISSTC), a member of the AAAI Program Committee of the top conference on artificial intelligence, and a reviewer of many CCF recommended journals and top conferences at home and abroad. His main research interests are AI large model security, multimodal deep learning, and optimization algorithms in machine learning. In the past 5 years, he has published more than 30 papers on AI security in top conferences such as ICCV, ECCV, AAI, IEEE TIFS, IEEE TNNLS, ACM TOPS, ACM TKDD, Journal of Software, Computer Research and Development, and Acta Electronica Sinica. 7 authorized invention patents; He has presided over the innovation special zone project of the Science and Technology Commission of the Central Military Commission (national level), the National Natural Science Foundation of China (general) project, the Natural Science Foundation of Zhejiang Province (key) project, and the natural science foundation of Zhejiang Province (general project). He has carefully supervised and trained 18 graduate students, 4 of whom continue to study for doctoral degrees.
